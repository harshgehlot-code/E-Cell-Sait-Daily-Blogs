About E-Cell SAIT

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) at SAIT is a student-run organization dedicated to fostering an entrepreneurial spirit within the college community. We believe that every student has the potential to innovate and create impact.

Our mission is to provide a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to learn, connect, and grow. We organize workshops, speaker sessions, pitch competitions, and networking events to equip students with the skills and mindset needed to succeed in the startup world.

Join us in building a vibrant ecosystem where ideas are celebrated, and dreams are turned into ventures!

Our Vision

To be the catalyst for innovation and entrepreneurship at SAIT, empowering students to become job creators rather than just job seekers.

What We Do

  • Host regular workshops on business planning, marketing, and funding.
  • Organize guest lectures from successful entrepreneurs and industry experts.
  • Facilitate mentorship opportunities with experienced professionals.
  • Conduct inter-college startup competitions and hackathons.
  • Provide resources and support for student-led initiatives.
